About Batak Spice Haven.

Batak Spice Haven is Indonesian restaurants, especially in the North Sumatra area, which you can find after 10 minutes walk from Koenji station and 5 minutes from Shin-Koenji station. Batak Spice Flavors, as the name suggests, has a very strong Batak nuance and sells food with authentic North Sumatran flavors. One of the mainstay menus is Mie Gomak, don't forget the lappet and the typical Batak andaliman sambal which are really delicious! One food package is sold for only 800 yen.
Those of you who miss village cuisine, especially in North Sumatra, can come and enjoy the dishes served which are rich in Batak spices here.
